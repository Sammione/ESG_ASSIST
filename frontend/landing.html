Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers. You can always reach out to ask about custom use cases, ESG frameworks or integrations.

Does the assistant hallucinate or make up data?

The ESG assistant is built with a RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) approach. It answers based only on the uploaded report, and is instructed to say “not disclosed” when data is not clearly present in the document.

Which ESG frameworks can it help with?

Today the assistant recognises SDGs, GRI, SASB and IFRS S1/S2. It can highlight when these are mentioned or partially covered in the report, and surface relevant sections you can review.

Is this suitable for Africa-focused ESG work?

Yes. The assistant is designed with emerging markets and African sustainability contexts in mind — including energy, infrastructure, agriculture and financial inclusion.

Can I use this in client work?

Absolutely. Many users treat the assistant as a first-pass analyst: it reads the report, surfaces metrics and risks, and you apply your professional judgement on top.
